Help / About

How to play: Jump over incoming rocks. Tap the canvas or Jump, or press Space/Up. You can double-jump. The game speeds up over time.

Accessibility: High contrast UI, large tap targets, keyboard support, and reduced motion is respected. Screen reader status announces score and game over.

Controls: Jump (Space/Up/Click), Pause/Resume (P), Reset clears best score only.

Made to run offline in any modern browser.
